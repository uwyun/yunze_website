Elden Ring

type of play:

Mystic Action Role-Playing Game

Game producer:

Hidetaka Miyazaki and George R.R. Martin.

Release date:

February 25th, 2022

brief Introduction of the content:

This game tells a story that takes place in a border area.Here, people surround the eternal queen Marica and receive her blessings. Those who are blessed will have a golden glow in their eyes.However, some people may lose their blessings for certain reasons. However, some people may lose their blessings for certain reasons.The golden light in their eyes would fade away when the blessings ceased, and eventually they would be expelled from the border area. Later, the core of the Blessing Ring of Elden was destroyed. The demigods launched several wars due to the contamination of the Blessing, and the Fade Outers regained the Blessing. The players will assume the role of the Fade Outer to collect the Elden Ring fragments, engage in a battle with the demigods, and eventually become the King of the Borderlands.
